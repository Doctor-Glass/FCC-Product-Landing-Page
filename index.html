The 2005 Subaru Baja

The Perfect Car

What Is the Subaru Baja?

God's perfect driving machine. The exceptional cross between a sedan and a pickup truck. Sporty in the front, utility in the back. All the bed space and towing capacity you'll ever need, with a 2.5L boxer engine providing an unparalleled driving experience.

Why Should I Buy a Subaru Baja?

Do you want to take the four-wheeler into the woods on Saturday and the family to church on Sunday? Drop your kids off at school, on the other side of a mountain? Haul moderate loads less than three and a half feet long (5' with optional bed extender)?

Do you possess a right foot and a pulse?

The 2005 Subaru Baja is the car for you.
